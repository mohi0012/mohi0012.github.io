About Me

Hello! My name is Tultul but you can call me Tula. I am a student at Algonquin College studying Interactive Media Design. I have also taken Business Management and Entrepreneurship. I decided to take IMD after practicing design since I was in middle school. I am also a mother to a 2 year old boy!

I would also consider myself as a writer. I have my own website that I blog on and upload photos on which focus on my life as a whole. Some of my hobbies consists of writing, cooking, yoga and meditation sessions, reading and designing logos or cover arts for friends and family members.
